How this works
  • Mistake data is pulled live from your tracker. Pages with ≤ 2 active mistakes are 🟢 strong; 3–4 are 🟡 medium; 5+ are 🔴 weak.
  • Strong pages: recite by heart — mushaf closed. Then go back and fix the specific ayāt with mistakes.
  • Medium / weak pages: open mushaf first → read slowly → close → recite by heart. 2–3 passes.
  • Manual override: tap any page to toggle it. A purple glow means it's been manually overridden.
  • Auto-sync: hit "Refresh from Tracker" anytime after a revision session to re-classify pages.
  • More pages memorised: increase Total Pages above — the schedule recalculates instantly.
